Best Procurement Strategies Deployed by Skechers
01
Global Sourcing from Asia
Low manufacturing cost, high scalability, skilled footwear suppliers, and strong material availability. China, Vietnam, Cambodia produce majority of Skechers footwear.
02
China+1 Diversification
Reduces risk from tariffs, geopolitical tensions, and over-reliance on one country. Production shifted to Vietnam, Cambodia, Indonesia.
03
Third-Party Manufacturing Model
No owned factories. Enables flexibility, lower capital investment, and rapid scaling for new product lines through contract manufacturing in Asia.
04
Material Innovation (EVA, Memory Foam)
Supports brand differentiation (comfort-based products) and quality consistency. Memory Foam insoles, lightweight EVA midsoles widely used.
05
Supplier Compliance & Audits
Ensures ethical production, reduces risk, and aligns with global consumer expectations for ESG. Regular factory audits and supplier code of conduct.
06
Efficient Logistics & Distribution
Reduces delivery time and cost; supports fast replenishment for global demand. Distribution centers in the US, Europe, Japan, and China.
07
Nearshoring for North America
Faster shipping, reduced logistics cost, and improved responsiveness to US market demand. Selective sourcing from Mexico for quick-turn models.
08
Digital Supply Chain & Forecasting
Improves planning accuracy, manages inventory risk, and enhances procurement decisions. Use of demand forecasting tools and real-time tracking.
